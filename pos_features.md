# Point of Sale (POS) System — Feature Overview

A comprehensive POS with expenses and stock management covers 6 core modules spanning sales, customer relations, inventory, and back-office finance.

---

## 1. Point of Sale (Checkout)

The customer-facing terminal that powers every transaction.

- Fast item search and barcode scanning
- Multiple payment methods — cash, card, mobile money
- Split payments and layaway support
- Discounts, coupons, and promotional pricing
- Receipt printing and email delivery
- Offline mode — sales continue without internet

---

## 2. Customer Management

Build relationships and drive repeat business.

- Customer profiles with full purchase history
- Loyalty points and rewards redemption
- Credit accounts and balance tracking
- Purchase trend analysis per customer
- SMS and email notifications for promotions and receipts

---

## 3. Cash Register

Keep every shift accountable and transparent.

- Opening and closing float management
- Drawer reconciliation at end of shift
- Shift summaries per cashier
- Void and refund tracking with audit trail
- Multi-till and multi-cashier support

---

## 4. Stock Management

Real-time inventory control from receiving to sale.

- Real-time inventory tracking — every sale auto-deducts stock
- Low-stock alerts and configurable reorder points
- Stock transfers between branches or warehouses
- Batch and expiry date tracking (ideal for food, pharma, cosmetics)
- Supplier management and purchase order creation
- Stock adjustments and write-off recording

---

## 5. Expense Management

Track everything going out to understand true profitability.

- Log and categorize business expenses
- Attach receipt images and supplier invoices
- Recurring and scheduled expense entries
- Expense approval workflows for team accountability
- Budget tracking with variance alerts
- Profit and loss view combining revenue and expenses

---

## 6. Reports & Analytics

Data-driven decisions across sales, stock, and finance.

- Daily, weekly, and monthly profit & loss statements
- Top-selling products and slow-moving stock reports
- Stock movement and adjustment history
- Cashier and shift performance summaries
- Tax and VAT summaries for compliance
- Export to Excel and PDF

---

## Summary

| Module | Key Benefit |
|---|---|
| Point of Sale | Fast, flexible checkout for any payment type |
| Customer Management | Loyalty, history, and targeted communication |
| Cash Register | Shift accountability and till reconciliation |
| Stock Management | Real-time inventory with reorder automation |
| Expense Management | Full cost visibility and budget control |
| Reports & Analytics | Unified P&L, stock, and performance insights |

---

*A well-integrated POS connects all six modules so that every sale automatically updates inventory, feeds into financial reports, and contributes to customer records — eliminating manual data entry and reducing errors.*
